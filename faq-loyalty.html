Frequently Asked Questions

What are Loyalty Coins?
Loyalty Coins (🏅) are GCCPROs' reward currency. You earn them by contributing accurate data to the database — either correcting existing records or adding new GCC companies. Coins have real monetary value: 1 coin = ₹5.

Is there a limit on how many coins I can earn?
Yes. You can earn a maximum of 1,000 coins per calendar month through contributions (corrections and new GCC submissions). This cap applies to earned coins only — purchased coins are not subject to this limit. The cap resets on the 1st of each month.
What is Power User status? ⚡ Power User
Power User is a premium 24-hour mode you can activate by spending 200 coins. While active, all your daily limits are doubled:

What is Top Voice status? ⭐ TOP VOICE
The member with the highest total coin balance across the entire GCCPROs community is designated as Top Voice. This badge appears next to their name in the leaderboard and on their profile. The ranking is live and updates automatically as coins are earned.
What is the Leaderboard?
The Leaderboard shows the top 10 members by total coin balance. Member emails are masked (e.g. sh***@company.com) to protect privacy. Click the 🏆 button in the top navigation to view it anytime. Your own entry is highlighted in blue.

Do coins expire?
Yes. Each coin batch expires 60 days from the date it was earned or purchased. For example, coins earned on 1 March expire on 30 April. Expired coins are automatically deducted from your balance. You will receive an email reminder 7 days before any coins are due to expire, so you have time to use them. Coins spent on Power User activation are consumed immediately and are not subject to expiry.
How are coins tracked? Is it transparent?
Every coin credit and debit is recorded in a ledger with a timestamp, action type, and reference (company name or payment ID). Your running balance is computed from this ledger. Coins earned from contributions are always tied to an approval event that the admin controls — there is no way to self-award coins.
I submitted a GCC — when do I get my coins?
Coins are credited only when the admin reviews your submission and types Approved in the status column. You will receive an email notification confirming the approval and the coins credited, along with your updated balance. If the monthly cap has been reached, the email will note how many coins were actually credited.
My submission was rejected — what happens?
No coins are credited for rejected submissions. The admin may contact you if the submission needs clarification before it can be accepted.
